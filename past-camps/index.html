What we've done so far

Summer Camping
all over the world

From 20 friends in the Austrian Alps to almost 60 Campers with a private lake in Cape Town: This is how Nomad Summer Camp grew from a stupid idea into something we can't stop doing.

Camp 1

Camp Bädäss

2023 · Bad Aussee, Austria

20 campers

The OG. The one that started it all. Nobody knew what to expect. Everybody left wanting to do it again. Also, someone ate a slug. We don't talk about it.

Camp 2

Camp Jandala

2024 · Novi Sad, Serbia

26 campers

Bigger, wilder, and somehow even more Camp. A location straight out of a coming-of-age movie, perched above the Danube with a backdrop that made every activity feel cinematic. The group was growing and so was the chaos.

Camp 3

Camp Chido

2024 · Cuernavaca, Mexico

18 campers

A private hacienda, an incredible pool, and the birth of two legends: Jeff the Camp mascot and the Bad Vibes Ghost, a mysterious figure whose destiny was to be chased away with good vibes.

Camp 4

Camp Chateau

2025 · Normandy, France

22 campers

We had a Camp in an actual castle! Chateau Coliving in Normandy opened its gates and we filled it with Camp games, Camp chaos, and the return of the Bad Vibes Ghost, who apparently followed us from Mexico.

Camp 5 · Most recent

Camp Big Daddy

2026 · Cape Town, South Africa

56 campers

Our biggest camp yet. And the one that proved this thing has no ceiling. 56 Campers from across the world, a private lake, a beach, and three days of pure magic. Camp Big Daddy set the bar so high we had no choice but to come back. Which is exactly what we're doing in 2027.
