About NSC

We're a group of full-time nomads who wanted to create the world's best grown-up summer camp—somewhere wild and unforgettable, for people who live life off the beaten path.

The Story

Nomad Summer Camp started as a wild idea between a group of friends who were tired of typical "nomad events"—coworking sessions and networking happy hours. We wanted something different. Something that made you feel like a kid again.

So we rented out a campground, rounded up a few dozen nomads, and put together three days of pure, unbridled fun. The result? People cried when it ended. And we knew we had to do it again.

Our Mission

To create a space where location-independent people can truly disconnect from their laptops, reconnect with their inner child, and build the kind of friendships that last a lifetime.

Every camp is hosted in a different corner of the world—because if you're going to gather nomads, the destination should be worth the journey.

Who We Are

The founders are all full-time nomads who've spent years working remotely from every corner of the globe. We've built businesses from beaches in Bali, cafés in Lisbon, and co-working spaces in Medellín. Nomad Summer Camp is our love letter to the nomad community.
